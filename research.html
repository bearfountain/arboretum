Hi! Great news for the Arboretum. We have funding from the Geneseo Foundation that is supporting many students doing research. If you want to help by getting involved with this research or want to help support research please contact us (see below)! The researchers are:

  • Pam McKernan ('01): Mapping breeding bird territories (Spring 2000).
  • Sarah Dagrosits & Simon Mc Caffery are developing an ecology course for 4th + 5th graders (Spring 2000).
  • Kristina Fanghanel is analyzing soil texture and soil invertebrate diversity and abundance (Spring 2000).
  • Jen Caito ('01): Mapping the Roemer Arboretum using Geographical Position System (GPS) satellite technology and Geographical Information Software (GIS) (1999-2000).
  • Christian Chiarelli ('01): Analyzing the abundance and diversity of plants in the Arboretum. Christian's also working on a virtual herbarium.
  • Maura Wixted ('01): Competition of two plant species for soil resources in the Arboretum.
